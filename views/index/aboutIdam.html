About Uma Arora, CEO

Facilitation. Coaching. Enablement.

Uma is an organization development professional who has had two careers: One with a focus on business and operations for first over 15 years and the second career which is her passion over the last decade where she is focused on helping individuals and organizations learn and develop to enhance their performance and productivity. She is currently the chief facilitator of Idam Learning, an India based firm focused on helping enterprises in enhancing performance, managing change, developing leaders, managing diversity and creating enabling cultures. She has extensive experience in talent development, Change facilitation, Executive coaching, helping startups develop vision and Strategy Prior to starting her consulting career with Idam Learning, she worked with corporates such as Daksh (a part of IBM), Digital, Sylvan Prometric and Aptech. During these years, she managed diverse portfolios eg. franchise network management, new business launch for Aptech, country operations management for Sylvan Prometric, Talent resourcing and Training Management in Daksh.

Uma blends her passion and curiosity for learning with knowledge of applied behavioral sciences. She draws from her years of business experience to create a results and application focused interdisciplinary approach to learning.

She believes in the inherent capacity of individuals to learn, grow and change.
